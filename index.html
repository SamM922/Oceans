"Water and air, the two essential fluids on which

all life depends, have become global garbage cans."

- Jacques Cousteau, Oceanographer

Oceans cover 71% of the earth's surface. Trade, travel, nourishment and protection are just a few of the things that oceans provide to billions of people everyday. However, as the human population continues to grow at an exponential rate, the world's oceans are facing growing problems such as ocean acidification, overfishing, and pollution. Our mission is to spread awareness about the ocean and marine issues that impact the Bay Area. Our priority is to provide readers with an understanding of what causes ocean pollution and solutions for one of Earth's most crucial problems.

Ocean Conservancy is a non-profit on the front lines of ocean protection, investing in research, leadership and advocacy that advances science-based solutions that help our oceans. Ocean Conservancy continues to develop innovative solutions to save our ocean.
